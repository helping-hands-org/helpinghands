Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Simply select a service, fill in your details, choose your preferred date and time, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately.

Are the professionals verified?

Yes, all professionals on our platform go through thorough background checks, identity verification, and skill assessments to ensure quality and safety.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 100% money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the service. Refunds are processed within 7 business days.

Is there 24/7 customer support?

Yes, our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or concerns about your booking.

Can I reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can reschedule your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled service. Contact our support team for assistance.

How are payments processed?

All payments are secure and encrypted. We accept credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets through our secure payment gateway.
